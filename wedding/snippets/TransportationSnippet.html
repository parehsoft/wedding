Organizácia / Organization:

 Autobus pôjde z ubytovania (Újezd u Brna) 12:40 do miesta hostiny (Jiříkovice), kde naberie ďaľších hostí a odtiaľ o 13:05 do katedrály. Z katedrály po gratuláciách do Jiříkovic na hostinu.

For international guests:
 Bus will board You at place of feast (Jiříkovice) and take You from there 13:05 to cathedral. After the mass and gratulations You will be taken by guide to the feast (back to Jiříkovice) to be there on 18:00, by other means of transportation.
